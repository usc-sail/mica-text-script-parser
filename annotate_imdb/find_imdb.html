Task

You will be given the IMSDb url of a movie script. Your task is to find the IMDb url of the corresponding movie.

Example

Given the IMSDb url 127_script, click on the link and open the page shown in Figure 1. Observe that the title of the movie is 127 hours and it is written by Simon Beaufoy and Danny Boyle. Copy the title 127 Hours to the Title field.

Search for 127 hours in IMDb, and find this 127_movie page, shown in Figure 2. Compare the graphic, title and writers of the script and the IMDb page, and check if they match. It matches in this case, so you copy the IMDb page url to the IMDb field.

The graphic and/or the names of the screenplay writers might be absent. You can then click on the Read Script link in the IMSDb page, and match the characters found in the script with the CAST section of the IMDb page.
